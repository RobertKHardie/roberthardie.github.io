About Me

I am a mechanical engineering graduate from the British Columbia Institute of Technology with hands-on experience in designing, analyzing, and building real hardware.

Recent work includes:

  • Designing a structural motor mount and load-bearing assembly for an electric conversion of a Reynard IndyCar chassis.
  • Developing a temperature-controlled test fixture for a carbon capture process, including sensor integration, control, and testing.

I enjoy projects that move from requirements and constraints, through CAD and simulation, into fabrication and verification. I am currently pursuing EIT-level roles in mechanical design, testing, and product development in British Columbia.
